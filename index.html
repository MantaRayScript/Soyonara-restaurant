about us

In the heart of Berlin, by the Großer Tiergarten park, you'll find a Japanese sushi restaurant with a rich history. Founded in the early 1990s by a group of Japanese chefs, the restaurant quickly gained a reputation for serving some of the best sushi in town. Today, it's a beloved institution in Berlin's culinary scene, offering fresh and delicious sushi, an elegant atmosphere, and impeccable service.

What sets this restaurant apart is its commitment to quality and authenticity. From traditional nigiri and sashimi to creative maki rolls and fusion dishes, all the ingredients are sourced from trusted suppliers and prepared with traditional techniques and a modern twist. Whether you're a sushi connoisseur or a newcomer to Japanese cuisine, come and experience the taste of Japan in the heart of Berlin.

our team

ceo takumi

The CEO of Soyonara, a Japanese sushi restaurant in Berlin, started as a sushi master when he opened the restaurant. However, as the restaurant became increasingly popular and attracted more customers, he shifted his focus to managing the business and passed on the role of sushi master to a younger chef. Despite this change, the restaurant has maintained its commitment to quality and authenticity, and continues to be a beloved institution in Berlin's culinary scene.

sushi master yuto

The main sushi master, who had trained under the CEO for years, was more than ready to take on the challenge. With a deep passion for the art of sushi-making and a commitment to using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, he has taken the restaurant's sushi to new heights of excellence. Under his leadership, the restaurant has continued to attract sushi lovers from all over the city and beyond. With his skill and dedication, the new sushi master ensures that Soyonara remains a top destination for authentic Japanese cuisine in Berlin.

sushi master hiro

At Soyonara, a Japanese sushi restaurant in Berlin, the junior sushi master complements the staff with his fresh perspective and creative approach. Although he is younger than the previous sushi master, he brings a wealth of experience and a willingness to experiment with new flavors and techniques. His innovative sushi creations have become a hit with customers, who appreciate his skillful blending of traditional and modern flavors. With his dedication to quality and creativity, the junior sushi master is an essential part of the Soyonara team, helping to keep the restaurant at the forefront of Berlin's culinary scene.

kitchen chef jiro

Jiro, is a master in the art of preparing Japanese cuisine. He has honed his skills over many years of training in Japan, and his dishes are renowned for their authenticity and flavor. Although customers can't see him working behind the scenes in the kitchen, they can taste the quality of his dishes with every bite. Jiro's specialty is ramen, and he has a secret recipe that he inherited from his grandfather. He puts great care and attention into every bowl of ramen, and it shows in the rich, savory broth and perfectly cooked noodles. Despite the many imitators that have sprung up around Berlin, no one can match Jiro's mastery of this classic Japanese dish.

sweets master yoko

Yoko is known for her exquisite and authentic Japanese sweets. She had inherited all her recipes from her grandmother, who had passed down her love and knowledge of sweets-making to her. Every day, Yoko would spend hours in the kitchen, carefully crafting each mochi with precision and care. Her dedication and attention to detail were evident in the taste of her treats, which were always a hit with customers. Yoko's mochi and dorayaki were not just delicious, they were also a reminder of the love and traditions that had been passed down through generations, and her customers could taste the history and heritage in every bite.

cocktail master miles

After studying Japanese language and culture in college, Miles decided to move to Japan to immerse himself further and learn all about the art of mixology. He spent years working in bars and mastering the craft of creating exquisite Japanese cocktails, from classics like the Tokyo Tea and Sake Martini to lesser-known gems like the Shochu Sour and Ume Highball. Now back in his hometown, Miles brought his expertise and passion to Soyonara, where customers were always impressed by the authenticity and quality of his creations. Miles' love of Japan and its culture was evident in every drink he made, and his customers could taste the dedication and care in each sip.

mamasan lena

Lena is a young woman with a passion for all things Japanese. She is studying Japanese Studies in college and loves immersing herself in the language and culture of Japan. Her love for Japanese pop music, or J-pop, is infectious, and she often sings along to the restaurant's playlist while serving customers. Lena's favorite food is sushi, and she never miss an opportunity to recommend her favorite rolls to customers. Her infectious smile and bubbly personality brings joy to everyone who walks through the doors of Soyonara, and her love of Japanese culture shine through in everything she does. Lena is more than just a waitress, she is a cultural ambassador, and her enthusiasm and warmth make customers feel like they are a part of Japan's vibrant and fascinating culture.
